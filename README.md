# This2Them 

Cinephile's Hardest Game

Can you connect the dots between two actors? 

 Players are given two actors and must connect them by naming films they share with co-stars, hopping from one actor to the next until they reach the target. Every move is validated in real time against the TMDB database — no guessing, no honor system.

Visit: https://this2them.netlify.app/ To play the game
